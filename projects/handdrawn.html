Hand Drawn

Drawings of hands by a learner



I was once told that the hardest thing to draw is the human hand. So the thinking was, if I got good at the hardest thing the rest should be easy, right? This is the result.


The drawings were made in one sitting, looking at a reference either on screen or paper in front of me.


I posted a drawing regardless of whether I thought it came out well or not and occasionally I included a comment about the drawing, the process, etc.


The hands are mostly of known/famous people and are, in one way or another, a hero of mine.
