Hand Drawn

Drawings of hands by a learner



I was once told, a long time ago, and have heard it many times since, that the hardest thing to draw is the human hand. So the thinking is, if I get good at the hardest thing the rest should be easy, right? We shall see.


The drawings are made in one sitting, looking at a reference either on screen or paper in front of me.


I post a drawing regardless of whether I think it came out well or not and occasionally I might include a comment about the drawing, the process, etc.


Feel free to guess the owner of the hand(s). Often they are known/famous people and are almost always, in one way or another, a hero of mine.
